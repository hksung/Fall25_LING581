# Required Sections

1. **Introduction**
   Present the research motivation, problem, and objectives.
2. **Related Work**
   Summarize prior studies; include findings from your background research.
3. **Method / Approach**
   Explain your proposed method, data, or analytical framework.
4. **Experiments**
   Describe datasets, experimental setup, and evaluation metrics.
5. **Results**
   Present and interpret findings with tables or figures.
6. **Conclusion**
   Summarize main points and future directions.
7. **Additional Sections (if relevant)**
   Limitations, Ethical Considerations, Acknowledgments, References.
